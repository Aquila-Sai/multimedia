Rotoscope Instructions

Step-by-step guide with visual references for your rotoscope project.

1
📷

Import Reference Footage

Import your video reference into After Effects and set up your composition.

2
📷

Create Roto Layer

Create a new layer above your footage for the rotoscope work.

3
📷

Draw Initial Mask

Use the pen tool to draw a mask around your subject on the first frame.

4
📷

Set Keyframes

Set keyframes for your mask points to begin the animation process.

5
📷

Trace Frame by Frame

Move through each frame, adjusting the mask to match the subject's movement.

6
📷

Refine Edges

Smooth out rough edges and ensure clean transitions between frames.

7
📷

Add Color & Style

Apply color fills and stylistic elements to your rotoscoped animation.

8
📷

Export & Render

Render your final animation and export it in your desired format.

📝 Notes

Production Notes

🎯 Project Goal

Create a 12-week rotoscope animation series tracing real footage frame-by-frame to master the fundamentals of rotoscoping.

⚙️ Software Used

Adobe After Effects for rotoscoping, with additional compositing and color grading in the later weeks.

⏱️ Time Investment

Each weekly assignment took approximately 4-6 hours of frame-by-frame tracing, cleanup, and rendering.

📚 Learning Outcomes

Mastered frame-by-frame animation, edge refinement, timing, and compositing integration through practical rotoscope exercises.
